My Services

Creating opportunities for self expression, business promotion, social connections, and anything else you can think of; via your laptops, tablets, and computer screens, using your choice of 6 key areas:

Content Creation

Not only can I build and design your website, but I can create the content for it. Using Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, SVG, and more I can add video, image, and audio to your website that entices your viewers.

UI Design

Database Design
